Rev Dr Sunday Gambo Chini


Pastor:UMCA CHAPEL TANKE

A Preacher and Teacher of God's Word

Rev Dr Sunday Gambo Chini an is Ordained Minister of the Gospel with the United Missionary Church of Africa (UMCA) precisely UMCA CHAPEL TANKE serving as the Pastor-in-Charge. Rev Dr Chini is C’lela by tribe from Zuru Local Government Area of Kebbi State. He is a trained Pastor and Teacher of the Word of God with over thirtys (30) years of working experience. He has also had the privilege of understudying Rev. Dr. ‘Gbenga and Adenike Odebiri for over Twenty-five (25) years. He is happily married To Mary Chini and their union is blessed with two children Ephraim Chini and Namsa Chini
